Arroz Con Pollo

Description:

Arroz Con Pollo is a reliable and classic latin dish that has fed many households. It's simplicity paradoxically highlights its complexity in preparation variation and seasoning options. It is an exceptional emulation of the best parts of being hispanic.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup long grain white rice
  • 2 cup chicken broth *see note
  • ½ cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables thawed if frozen or use canned
  • ½ onion chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic minced
  • 1 lb chicken thighs or chicken pieces of choice
  • ½-1 tsp cumin adjust to taste
  • s&p to taste
  • olive oil
