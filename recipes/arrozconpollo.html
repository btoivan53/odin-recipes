Arroz Con Pollo

Description:

Arroz Con Pollo is a reliable and classic latin dish that has fed many households. It's simplicity paradoxically highlights its complexity in preparation variation and seasoning options. It is an exceptional emulation of the best parts of being hispanic.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup long grain white rice
  • 2 cup chicken broth *see note
  • ½ cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables thawed if frozen or use canned
  • ½ onion chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic minced
  • 1 lb chicken thighs or chicken pieces of choice
  • ½-1 tsp cumin adjust to taste
  • s&p to taste
  • olive oil

Steps:

  1. Rinse rice in a sieve with cold water until water runs clear. This is highly recommend it to prevent rice from clumping and sticking together.
  2. In a large skillet over medium heat add olive oil and onion. Cook for a couple of minutes and add garlic.
  3. Season chicken with salt, pepper, and cumin. Add chicken to pan and sear until browned on both sides. Don't worry if chicken is not cooked through, it will continue to cook with rice. Remove chicken from pan and set aside.
  4. Add rice to pan and cook with onion and garlic until slightly toasted. Add in tomato sauce and vegetables. Pour in chicken broth and slightly stir mixture. Add salt as needed.
  5. Place chicken thighs back in pan on top of rice. Cover with lid and let it cook for about 30 minutes over medium heat. If liquid remains after 30 minutes, cook for additional 5 minutes until all liquid is absorbed. Turn off heat and leave covered for additional 10 minutes.
